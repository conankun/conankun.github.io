Junghyun Kim

Junghyun Kim is a graduate student at Georgia Institute of Technology, majoring in computer science. His research interest lies in theoretical computer science, especially in algorithm, optimization, complexity theory, data science, machine learning, cryptography, and high performance computing.

One of his biggest strength is a strong foundation in algorithm and mathematics. He participated in computer olympiad when he was in high school where he learned a number of algorithms beyond college course level and problem solving skills; he also majors in mathematics which bestowed a good foundation in mathematics upon him.

He is a really passionate about learning new concepts, collaborating with others, and approaching a problem in a different perspective from others.
