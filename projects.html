  • Mar 2014 - Current
    Project Manager and Developer; IT Factory COIN
    Website : http://algorithm.dimigo.hs.kr
    Created and managed program grader (online judge) for Korea Digital Media High School. In this system, instructors can assign programming problems to students and they should submit correct solution until the deadline specified by the instructor. After or before the deadline, the instructor can see statistics on students' submission, including mean,median, and standard deviation of the score of each problem and how many students submitted. Also, students can improve algorithm skills by solving numerous problems on the system. We encouraged this kind of learning by bestowing them a "badge" - there is a specific requirement to get a badge and if a user satisfies it, we grant that badge. We have approximately six hundred problems as of June 1st 2016. IT Factory COIN has been used for assignments (C Programming) for the entire pre-freshmen since 2015(every year). Now it's used as official online judge for C programming course at KDMH. I programmed front and back end of v1.0 and judging engine for v2.0 (current version).
  • Apr 2013 - Nov 2013
    Web-app Developer; WithStudy
    Created "WithStudy" Application in which students can schedule independent study time and manage study plans and extracurricular activities plans. One of our difference from other scheduling app is we have a bunch of textbook data in our database and students can utilize this data, enabling them to set study goal more effectively. For example, if a student uses WithStudy scheduling app and he or she is going to study SAT Practice Exam book practice No.1, he or she can mark practice No.1 part completed and keep track of overall progress on this book. We participated in SK Teen App Challenge and received honorable mention for being placed on within 20 places out of 400 teams.
